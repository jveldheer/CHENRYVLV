KEY TRAINING TERMS

  • RPE: Rate of Perceived Exertion. RPE 7 = ~3 reps left. RPE 10 = max
  • CNS: Your explosiveness system - central nervous system
  • Tempo 3-1-2: 3 seconds down, 1 second pause, 2 seconds up
  • Volume: Sets × Reps × Weight | Intensity: % of max

AUTO-ADJUST RULES (READ DAILY)

  • Sleep < 8.5 hr: Drop intensity 15%, add 20-30 min nap
  • New pain: Remove triggers, switch to mid-range iso 30-45s
  • Stress = 5: Full recovery day, breathwork twice, easy walk + mobility only
  • Weight swing > 5 lb: Push fluids + carbs + salt
